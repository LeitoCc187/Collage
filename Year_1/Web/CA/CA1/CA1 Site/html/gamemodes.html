    Elixius Lobby

    The Lobby is an area that you log into when you join the server. It works as a hub for users to move between different gamemodes and has a few mini-games that you can take part in to make money and have fun while you wait to join a gamemode.

    SMP

    SMP or Servival multiplayer is a open world gamemode with a large map to explore where players can fight and build mega bases and protect them or desty them if they so wish. We offer a large amount of opportunities for players who use our servers to have fun and we try mak ethe experience as enjoyable as possible.

    Firendly SMP

    Friendly SMP is a open world gamemode with a large map to explore where players can fight and build bases and protect them or destry them if they so wish. This server is the same as the SMP but it plays closer to the base game of minecraft with little added content.

    Skyblock

    Skyblock is a fun and firendly gamemode with lots of players and stuff to do. We offer lots of cool cosmentics and have a daily reward system in place to make sure you get the most from your time we include a daily quest system that woll create quests for each player at random everyday.

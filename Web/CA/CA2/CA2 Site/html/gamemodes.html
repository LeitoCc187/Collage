    OUR GAMES

    Elixius Lobby

    The Lobby is an area that you log into when you join the server. It works as a hub for users to move between different gamemodes and has a few mini-games that you can take part in to make money and have fun while you wait to join a gamemode.

    SMP

    SMP or Survival Multiplayer is an open-world gamemode with a large map to explore where players can fight, build mega bases, and either protect or destroy them as they wish. We offer a large amount of opportunities for players who use our servers to have fun and aim to make the experience as enjoyable as possible.

    Friendly SMP

    Friendly SMP is an open-world gamemode similar to SMP but closer to the base game of Minecraft. It includes minimal added content, making it a simpler and more familiar experience for players.

    Skyblock

    Skyblock is a fun and friendly gamemode with lots of players and activities. It offers cool cosmetics, a daily reward system, and a randomized daily quest system to keep the experience engaging for players.

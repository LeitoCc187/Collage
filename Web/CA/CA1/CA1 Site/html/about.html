    We just released the Elixius Server!

    Our team has worked endlessly to get this released on time and we are happy to announce that the server is open to publice use for all minecraft players.

    Come join our growing community and help us get to the top of the minecraft server world. We are open for suggestion and staff application going forward.

    OUR HISTORY

    Elixius has been around for over 3 and a half years now. What started off as a small community minecraft server has developed into something we couldn't even imagine.

    OUR STAFF

    The Elixius staff team are friendly, dedicated, and always online to help everything run smoothly. Have any questions? Make sure to do /staff ingame to reach out or make a ticket on our discord server!

    OUR GAMES

    We have SMP, Friendly SMP and Skyblock on our network, all with fully custom items and useful perks and features. Join today at mc.elixius.net!

    OUR SERVERS

    We run multiple servers using multiple computers and we use a system to manage all the servers and gives us easly access to change files and update the servers

    OUR GOALS

    We strive to create a happy enviorment for our community to grow and strive in. We look towards the future so that we can craft a interactive world to explore!

    OUR PROMISES

    We Promise fair treatment of all people, a safe enviorment for childen, high quality servers and games to enjoy with your firends!
